# Bonus & Increment Structure – Financial Year 2026/2027

**Date:** 14th May 2026  
**To:** All Staff  
**From:** Management  
**Subject:** Bonus & Increment Structure – Financial Year 2026/2027

Dear Team,

We would like to inform all employees regarding the bonus and increments structure applicable for the Financial Year 2026/2027.

---

## Bonus Eligibility & Calculation Criteria

### Company Profitability Requirement

The company must first achieve a **Net Profit (NP) exceeding 20%** in order for employees to be eligible for any annual bonus payout.

### Bonus Calculation Method

The bonus amount for each employee who has completed **one year of service by 31st March 2027** will be determined based on the following evaluation structure:

- Overall Company Net Profit (NP) Achievement
- Department Target Achievement
- Individual KPI Achievement

The final bonus entitlement will be calculated based on the combined performance outcome of the above three criteria.

### Two Months' Salary Bonus Eligibility

In the event the company achieves **more than 36% Net Profit (NP)**, all employees who have completed **one year of continuous service by 31st March 2027** will be eligible to receive a **two-months' salary bonus**.

---

## Salary Increment Criteria

Annual salary increments for the Financial Year 2026/2027 for the employees who have **got confirmed in their positions by 31st March 2027** will be evaluated and determined based on the following criteria:

- Overall Company Net Profit (NP) Achievement
- Department Target Achievement
- Individual KPI Achievement

Increment percentages and final approvals will be determined by management based on the overall business performance and individual contribution.

Management reserves the right to review and finalize all bonus and increment decisions based on the company's financial performance and operational requirements.

We thank all employees for their continued dedication, commitment, and contribution towards the company's success.
